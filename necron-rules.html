Awakening a Tomb World

A Necron tomb world’s awakening is a multi-stage process, empowering its systems and giving access to Master System abilities. Your Necron Crusade force will distribute power to these systems, gaining bonuses for battles.

Awakening Points

Your Crusade force starts with 3 Awakening Points to allocate across three Systems: Command, Reanimation, and Translocation. The total distribution of these points will unlock Master System abilities.

System Bonuses

Each System provides three unique bonuses. You can select a System bonus by completing the relevant Awakening Agenda and allocating an Awakening Point to it. Bonuses include enhancements to durability, reanimation abilities, or increased mobility.

Awakening Agendas

To allocate an Awakening Point, you must complete an Awakening Agenda. These are specialized objectives tied to each system. Completing an Agenda lets you choose a System bonus and allocate an Awakening Point to it.

Master Systems

Once all three Awakening Points are allocated, you unlock Master System abilities. Using these powerful abilities requires you to roll a D3 and remove that many Awakening Points, reducing your access to system bonuses.

Dyastic Awakening Systems

Command System

The Command System strengthens your control over your Necron army by reinforcing the neural links between your forces and the dynasty's leaders.

Awakening Agenda: Subjugation Decree

Each time your Warlord’s unit destroys an enemy unit, your Warlord gains 1XP. Gain 3XP to activate a Command System bonus and allocate 1 Awakening Point.

  • Harmonised Dynastic Hyperdermis: Increase Toughness by 1 for a selected CHARACTER unit for the entire battle.
  • Servile Preservation Protocols: Bodyguards in a unit can fight back after dying, on a 3+ roll.
  • Remastered Deficiency: Once per battle round, use the Command Re-roll Stratagem for 0CP for a Necron CHARACTER unit.

Command Master System Ability: Directive Override

Once per battle, choose between:

  • Reroll Battle-shock tests for Necron units during the Command phase.
  • Add 1 to Objective Control for Necron units in your army for the Command phase.

Reanimation System

Focused on enhancing the reanimation capabilities of Necron forces, the Reanimation System improves their ability to recover from damage.

Awakening Agenda: Immortal Servitude

Track each wound recovered through Reanimation Protocols. Every 12 wounds gives your Warlord 1XP (max 3XP). Gain 12 wounds to activate a Reanimation System bonus and allocate 1 Awakening Point.

  • Rapid Reanimation: Reroll a Reanimation Protocols roll once per battle round.
  • Resurrection Flux: Add 1 to Reanimation Protocols rolls for units Below Half-strength.
  • Anastasic Contingency: Once per battle, a unit can activate Reanimation Protocols when reduced below Starting Strength.

Reanimation Master System Ability: Contra-Mortis Protocol

Once per battle, choose between:

  • Use Reanimation Protocols twice in a turn for one unit.
  • Add 1 to Reanimation Protocols rolls during the Command phase for all units.

Translocation System

The Translocation System enhances Necron mobility by enabling dimensional travel across the battlefield.

Awakening Agenda: Relentless Expansion

Select up to three Necron units at the end of the battle that are within 6" of your opponent’s battlefield edge. Each gains 3XP. Activate a Translocation System bonus and allocate 1 Awakening Point if three or more units gain XP.

  • Astromantic Perception Portal: Re-roll Hit rolls for one unit that Deep Strikes.
  • Dimensional Catacombs: Up to three units gain the Scouts 6" ability and can Fly when using it.
  • Transtemporal Acceleration: Add 2 to Advance and Charge rolls for one unit once per battle.

Translocation Master System Ability: Spectral Relocation

Once per battle, choose between:

  • Swap two units from the battlefield and Reserves.
  • Redeploy up to three Necron units after both players have deployed.
