Hive Fleet Overview

The Tyranid Hive Fleet is an unstoppable force. Known for consuming entire worlds, their units specialize in biological warfare, adapting to any environment and overpowering enemies with sheer numbers.

Key Strengths:

  • Adaptability
  • Synaptic Control
  • Massive Numbers
