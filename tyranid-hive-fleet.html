Hive Fleet Red Shroud

Hive Fleet Red Shroud is a terrifying force, shrouded in shadow and silence. Known for its precision and brutality, this fleet strikes without warning, paralyzing enemies with fear before launching lethal assaults. Its bioforms are specialized for stealth and assassination, blending into the darkness before emerging to tear apart their prey.

Key Strengths:

  • Stealth and Infiltration
  • Psychological Warfare and Terror Tactics
  • Massive Monstrous Creatures
